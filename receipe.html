Kurma Puri

Ingredints Requried

  • Vegetables
    • Green Peas
    • CauliFlower
    • Carrot
  • Spices
  • Butter

Steps to Prepare the Dish

  1. Boil all the cut vegetables,adding little pinch of salt.
  2. After all the vegetables are boiled properly drain the water and store.
  3. Take 2 tablespoon of oil.
  4. Add the spices like clove,jeera,curry leaves.
  5. Add the masala prepared by the dry coconut powder and all the khada masalas and let the whole mixture fry for few minutes.
  6. Add all the boiled vegetables to mixture and cook it for 15 mins.
  7. Your dish is ready and can be served with puri.
  • Enjoy your dish.
